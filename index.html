Overview

KIAF ART SEOUL first opened in 2002 to develop the foundation of Korean art and introduce contemporary Korean art to the international audience. KIAF ART SEOUL estavlished itself as an international art fair where visitors can experience the globalized contemporary art and closely examine the trend of the art market.
